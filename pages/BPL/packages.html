Lomdei's BPL Program

Professional Development Packages

Package 1:
Foundations

This course is geared towards teachers who have not yet implemented Blended and Personalized Learning (BPL) methodology in their classes.

These teachers will gain the understanding and tools necessary to successfully run a BPL class on their own.

This year-long program includes workshops, one-on-one coaching, and participation in a community of like-minded educators to share ideas, experiences, and successes.

Package 2:
Building Blocks

This course is geared towards teachers who already have knowledge of BPL and experience implementing it in their classrooms. These teachers will build on their knowledge and experience to take the next steps in the successful implementation of BPL.

This course will focus on ensuring a student-centered classroom and specific implementation of all four core areas of Personalized Learning. The course is typically taken by 2nd year Lomdei BPL teachers or by teachers who have been trained in BPL in other venues and want to sharpen their skills and impact in their classrooms.

This year-long program includes workshops, one-on-one coaching, and participation in a community of like-minded educators sharing ideas, experiences, and successes.

Package 3:
In-House Coaching

This course trains a representative at your school to become a Lomdei BPL coach who will coach other teachers at your school. The coach will get training in the Google Challenge-Based Coaching Model (the same model that all Lomdei coaches follow). Your in-house coach will learn all the necessary methodologies to effectively coach teachers and oversee classroom observations.

There are 2 reasons to consider having someone on your staff train to be a Lomdei BPL coach:

  1. Having someone “on the ground'' who can consistently check in with teachers and observe their classes in person will advance the success of the teacher who is implementing the Lomdei BPL Project.
  2. Having an in-house coach will save on the school’s cost per teacher for the Lomdei BPL program. We will no longer need to provide a coach for each teacher.

Packages 1 and 2 Include:

Teacher Orientation

At the start of the course, teachers will participate in a 30-45 minute orientation Zoom session to help build a sense of community with the other participating teachers. During the session, they will meet the facilitator and the other Lomdei BPL teachers in their cohort and be introduced to the course objectives and expectations.

Principal Orientation

At the start of the course, principals will participate in a 30-45 minute orientation Zoom session. During their orientation, they will meet Mrs. Richmond and each other, and discuss their role in supporting their teachers, ensuring that course goals and objectives are met.

BPL Workshops

12 hours of remote workshops over Zoom, broken up into 3-4 sessions

In-person workshops are available - ask us for more details

One-on-one Coaching

6 hours of one-on-one coaching, broken up into 10 sessions of 35 minutes (the first sessions 45 minutes)

Principal Check-In

Mrs. Chani Richmond will meet with each principal 3 times over the course of the year to provide updates as to their teachers’ progress. She will use data from each coach to show the extent to which each teacher is progressing toward the goals of the course

Culmination Ceremony

A culmination ceremony for teachers and principals will be held over Zoom at the end of the school year. During the ceremony, educators will celebrate their successes by presenting specific achievements and describing the positive effects of BPL methodology on their students.

Package 3 Includes:

BPL Workshops (If Necessary)

12 hours of remote workshops over Zoom, broken up into 3-4 sessions

In-person workshops are available - ask us for more details

Coaching Workshops

6 hours of training in the methodologies of the Google Challenge-Based Coaching model. Participants will be trained to provide coaching sessions and classroom observation sessions.

One-on-one Coaching

One of our coaches will check in with the school's coach once a month to discuss strategies on advancing each of the teachers towards successful BPL in their classrooms.

To learn more about how Lomdei's BPL Program can benefit your school and teachers, please contact us.

Professional Development Packages

Our professional development will train a cohort of teachers over the course of a school year. A second cohort of teachers can be added in the following school year with the additional options below.

Basic Training Package

Training: 10 hours of training over 3 sessions during the first 4 months of school for the selected cohort of teachers.
Coaching/Mentoring: 8 half-hour monthly follow-up coaching/mentoring sessions for each teacher in the cohort

Advanced Training Package

Training: 10 hours of training over 3 sessions during the first 4 months of school for the selected cohort of teachers.
Coaching/Mentoring: 16 half-hour bi-monthly follow-up coaching/mentoring sessions for each teacher in the cohort The option of in-person meeting and classroom observations is available as well.
