Lomdei was created to promote and infuse the ideals of חנוך לנער על פי דרכו and with one purpose in mind: להגדיל תורה ולהאדירה. We are privileges to partner with over 100 schools through our Learning Platform Division and our PD & Coaching Division.

May we be zoche to continue providing the necessary training, tools and support to enhance the learning experience in our Mosdos Torah for every teacher and student.
