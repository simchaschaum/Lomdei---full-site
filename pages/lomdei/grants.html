Government Grants

for Lomdei Services

Title IIA and Title IV are federal grants, available for all schools in all States.

Schools can use their Title IIA dollars (PD) towards PD & Coaching.

Schools can use their Title IIA (coaching) and Title IV dollars (Software) towards the Lomdei Learning Platform (software).

For more information, please contact us.
