The Learning Platform:
Simple, Yet Powerful.

The Learning Platform Division, highly recommended and endorsed by Torah Umesorah (Click here to view Rabbi Nojowitz's letter), is aimed at delivering a simple yet powerful Limudei Kodesh educational tool focused on skill building, formative and summative assessments and rich data tracking for both teachers and students. With a reporting tool that tracks skills over time, the Platform can be used to facilitate standardized testing in your school. Lomdei will customize your skill-based needs in each grade, providing benchmarks and diagnostics in the skills your school wants to track.
