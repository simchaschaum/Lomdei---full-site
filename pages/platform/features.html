Lomdei Plaform Features

A Tool for Acquiring Knowledge, or for Formative & Summative Assessment

Students can work on the Lomdei Platform at the beginning of a unit, as a safe and interactive way to interact with an unseen text. After the material has been taught, students can also use the Plaform for chazara, and teachers can use it as a tool for both formative and summative assessments.

Additionally, the Lomdei Platform is an ideal tool to enable rebbeim and moros to teach a small group of students directly. While the rebbe or morah can give attention to their group, the other students are doing meaningful work on the Plaform that the teacher can check later.

Real-Time Rich Analytical Data

The Lomdei Platform, with its rich analytical data system, measures all of a student's achievements, regardless of age, grade or subject. Students get instant feedback on the work they are doing and teachers have a real-time view of their students' data. The Platform marks and collects the data in our reporting system; no marking required by the teacher!

Personalize Materials - Easy to Modify Content

Teachers can either use previously created content to send to students or they can easily customize the questions to best match the needs of their class. The updates and modifications automatically get updated in the studentss accounts.

Passive and Active Learning

Teachers can access lessons that have both passive learning including audio, video and reading sheets to help explain the lesson as well as active learning using formative and summative assessments.

Library of Material

The Lomdei Platform provides a massive library of ready to use limudei kodesh skill building assessments including Chumash, Navi, Mishna, Gemara, Yomim Tovim, Yedios Klalios, Ivrit and more.
